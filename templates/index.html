🚩 Common Scam Indicators

  • Urgency: Phrases like "Immediately", "Within 24 hours".
  • Unsolicited Jobs: High daily income for "liking videos".
  • APK Files: Never install .apk files sent via WhatsApp/SMS.
  • Digital Arrest: Police will NEVER video call you.

🛡️ How to Stay Safe

  • Verify Links: Only trust official domains.
  • Don't Share OTPs: No bank asks for OTP.
  • Ignore Unknown Calls: Avoid +92, +84 numbers.
